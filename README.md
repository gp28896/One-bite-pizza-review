“One Bite Pizza Review” is a viral internet series created by Dave Portnoy where he travels around trying pizza and rating it in a very specific, consistent style.


🍕 What “One Bite Pizza Review” actually is


It’s a simple but strict format:


Portnoy goes to a pizza shop (usually unannounced)

Orders a plain cheese slice (so every review is comparable)

Takes a bite and says:

👉 “One bite, everybody knows the rules” (his catchphrase)

Then gives a score out of 10 (with decimals)


👉 The goal: judge pizza purely on taste and basics, not hype.


⚖️ How the scoring works

Scale: 0.0 → 10.0

Decimals matter (e.g., 7.1, 8.3, 9.4)

Scores above 9 are extremely rare

Rough interpretation:

6–7 → decent / average

7–8 → good

8–9 → great

9+ → elite (best of the best)

google maps URLs of locations with scores >= 9: https://maps.app.goo.gl/BEV5D5ZZ1qTXNGR69

🍕 What he looks for in pizza


Portnoy focuses heavily on:


Crust / undercarriage (crispness, structure)

Sauce balance

Cheese quality

“No flop” test (slice shouldn’t droop too much)
